ABOUT

I'm a mechatronic engineer specialized in the field of robotics & control systems with experience in R&D. I'm interested in biollogically inspired robots including humanoid robots, prosthesis and mobile robots, with a major focus on developing practical prosthetic applications. I also have a strong interest in developing and implement nonlinear controllers in the context of mechanical systems. Additionally, I have practical background in mechanical design, electronics and embedded systems.
